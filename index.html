How It Works

Circadian Rhythms?

Read More

Circadian rhythms are physical, mental, and behavioral changes that follow a daily cycle. They respond primarily to light and darkness in an organism's environment. Sleeping at night and being awake during the day is an example of a light-related circadian rhythm. Optimizing these rhythms can reduce fatigue and improve productivity.

Luxe Wifi Hub

The Luxe Wifi Hub connects the sensors and lightblubs to your phone allowing you to change settings from where ever you are. It performs the ambient light optimization using a low power arduino board so your lights are on when need them but off when you don't. Connect to your Hub to your Wifi to enable app controls.

Luxe Ambient Light Sensors

The Luxe Ambient Light Sensors are battery powered, content-aware light sensors detect how much light is needed to light up a room. These sensors enable Luxe to save you a massive amount of energy by turning down the brightness when you don't need it. Stick the sensor to any flat surface with the adhesive backend!

Luxe Lightbulbs

Luxe Lightbulbs reduce energy usage through unique design. Luxe Lightbulbs combine yellow and blue LED to the wide range of sunlight colors. These lightbulbs create the full omnidirectional through refraction technlogy with the brightness you need to light your life.
